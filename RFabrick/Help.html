How does RFabrics select and present merchandise?

Merchandise is selected based on product uniqueness, quality, demonstrability, timeliness, and appeal. RFabrics is one of only a handful of retailers with its own in-house Quality Assurance program and extensive warehouse quality control. A full-time staff of stage designers, technicians, and stylists makes sure each product is presented in the most appropriate way. Finally, our Program Hosts, many of whom travel the world to research products, explain and demonstrate every item in an entertaining, informative, and friendly presentation.

What are the quality assurance and a product's country of origin?

Regardless of where an item originates, our quality inspectors travel to all of suppliers' to help ensure that all of the products meet RFabrics' highest quality standards. RFabrics' professional Quality Control team hand tests most of the products unit-by-unit carefully to ensure your satisfaction. New products are selected with reliability considerations and your expectations in mind, and also following a strict protocol and inspection requirement.

Can I Change My Order?

Unfortunately, we are unable to make any changes to an existing order. There is a short period of time in which an order may be cancelled. If your order can be cancelled, we encourage you to replace the order at your earliest convenience.

How do I cancel my order?

Due to our commitment to process orders quickly, there is a short period of time in which you may cancel your order. You may choose to cancel your entire order or a portion of your order as long as the order has not yet been 'Dispatched' for fulfillment.
If you order has not been shipped, you may cancel it for a full refund.
